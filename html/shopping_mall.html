Rego Park is home to some of Queens' most popular shopping destinations, including the 4-floor Rego Center (25,700 m2).
It has Marshalls, Burlington Coat Factory, Old Navy, and (a newly opened) IKEA locations, as well as a multilevel parking garage developed by Vornado Realty Trust.
